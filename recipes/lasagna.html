The Lasagna Dish

Description

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish made with layers of wide, flat pasta sheets, rich meat or vegetable sauce, creamy béchamel or ricotta cheese, and melted mozzarella, all baked to perfection. Hearty and flavorful, lasagna is a comforting, oven-baked meal that's popular around the world for its satisfying blend of textures and savory taste.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna pasta sheets
  • Ground beef (or mixed ground meat)
  • Crushed tomatoes or tomato sauce
  • Ricotta cheese (or béchamel sauce)
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Onion and garlic
  • Olive oil
  • Salt & Pepper

Steps of the Recipe

  1. Prepare the Meat Sauce
    • Heat olive oil in a pan.
    • Add onion and garlic, sauté until golden.
    • Add ground meat and cook until browned.
    • Stir in tomato sauce, seasoning, salt, and pepper.
  2. Prepare the Béchamel Sauce
  3. Pre-cook the Lasagna Sheets (if required)
  4. Layer the Lasagna
  5. Bake
  6. Rest and Serve
